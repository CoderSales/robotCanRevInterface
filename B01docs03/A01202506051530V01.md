### Protocol for setting up Jetson locally using Linux

| Command                                      | Action         | Motor Controller |
|---------------------------------------------|----------------|------------------|
| `cansend can0 02050098#0000803E00000000`     | forward        | FL 24            |
| `cansend can0 02050098#0000800000000000`     | stop (abrupt)  | FL 24            |
